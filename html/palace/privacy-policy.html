Last Updated: January 2026

Nawalgarh Properties is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our platform, we may collect the following information:

  • Name and contact details (phone, email)
  • Property requirements and preferences
  • Website usage data (pages visited, time spent)
  • Device and browser information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Suggesting properties based on your requirements
  • Contacting you and resolving your queries
  • Improving our website
  • Sending relevant property updates and offers (only with your permission)

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third party, except in these cases:

  • When you make a property enquiry - your contact may be shared with the seller
  • When required by law
  • With your explicit permission

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, 100% security cannot be guaranteed on the internet. Please keep this in mind when sharing your information.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies that are stored in your browser. These cookies help improve the website experience. You can disable cookies from your browser settings, however some features may not work as a result.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Right to view your stored information
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to request deletion of your information
  • Right to opt-out of marketing communications

7. WhatsApp Communication

When you message us on WhatsApp, you give us permission to communicate with you. We will only use your number for property-related communication.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes become effective once published on the website. Continued use of the platform means you agree to the updated policy.
